Windows 8

If you are a making a Windows 8 game, you should assign functions to the App Bar following these simple rules:

  • Limit the number of commands to avoid the app bar from looking complicated.
  • Choose icons that are easy to understand or predict.
  • Keep text labels short (use the tooltip to convey more information on hover)
  • Place persistent commands on the right
  • If there are a larger number of commands, separate distinct command sets on the left or the right to balance out the app bar.
