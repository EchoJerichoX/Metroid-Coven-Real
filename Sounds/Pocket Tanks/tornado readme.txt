Description:

15 weapons for use with Pocket Tanks Deluxe


Weapons List:

* [new] - means that brand-new effects are being used thanks to v1.2 update

Tornado - Cruises along ground, damages terrain, and sends tanks flying
Thor's Hammer - Lightning strikes tank from flying hammer [new]
Stampede - "Herd" of cruisers cover the ground, bouncing tanks around
Hot Coals - Black char bursts into flames and bouncing hot coals
Fizz Bombs - Caustic smoke is released from bombs that fizz and pop about [new]
Graviton - Bullets attack tank, pounding it in 'all' directions... my favorite! [new] 
Shield - Bouncy arc forms over your tank, leaving room to fire
Firefly - Looks like a calm summer night, and about as dangerous
Glue Storm - Coats the entire terrain with glue
Glue Gun - Fires burning glue when bullet nears enemy tank
Run 'n' Gun - Bullet bounces and rapid fires directly at enemy tank [new]
Scorcher - Massive fire ball flies through air and covers ground [new] 
Saucer Attack - Call down two flying saucers on the enemy tank [new]
Tractor Beam - Drags tanks out from far underground and into the sky [new]
Smoke Bomb - Releases caustic smoke and messes with controls [new]
